## What's new in this release (v4.0.2)

### UI Refactoring & Performance Upgrades

This update brings a significant structural change to how friends and group chats are managed, along with performance upgrades across the entire application interface.

- **List Management Hub**: The 'Friends' page has been completely redesigned into a unified 'List' tab. The interface now features a tab switcher (identical to the Dashboard) to separate your Direct Messages and Group Chats into two distinct lists.
- **Contextual Management**: The manual "Group Chat" toggle switch has been removed. Tapping 'Add' on the Friends tab will automatically prompt for a username, while tapping 'Add' on the Groups tab will automatically prompt for a group name.
- **Contextual Actions**: Action buttons like "Clear" are now scoped to your current view. Clearing the Friends tab will wipe out DMs without touching your groups, and vice versa.
- **Snappier Interface**: Removed all micro-animations and transition delays across the application. Menus, tabs, and buttons now respond instantly with zero artificial lag, providing a much faster user experience.
